About Me

I'm Joscha, I develop software and hardware tools for scientific research. These tools are designed to facilitate experimental control, data acquisition, analysis, and management. Currently, I work for the Department of Biology and Chemistry at University of Bremen, Germany, as a research software and systems engineer (RSSE).

Technologies

I have experience working with a wide range of technologies, from C++ and custom electronics for interactive audio-visual stimulation to Python and MATLAB for data pipelines and high-performance computing. I have also worked with web and database systems to create comprehensive solutions for scientific research.
